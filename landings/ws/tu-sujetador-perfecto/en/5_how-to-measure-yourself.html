"80% of women don’t wear the right size bra.
Are you one of them?"

Step 1
Measure your bust with a measuring tape just above the nipples (A).

Step 2
Measure around your chest just under your bust (B). Check the table to find where the two measurements meet (A+B) and you’ll discover your size.

It’s important to take these measurements without clothes on and when you are not on your period, as your bust and belly can be bigger on those days.

And remember, wearing the right size isn’t just healthier, it also lifts and stylizes your bust and improves your posture.
